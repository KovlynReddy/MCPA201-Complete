About Us

Imported JDM and German Cars | Crate Motors is a car dealership that specializes in the import and sale of Japanese Domestic Market (JDM) and German cars, as well as crate motors.

Our team is made up of car enthusiasts who have a passion for high-performance vehicles. We carefully select each car and motor that we import, ensuring that our customers receive the best quality and performance.

Our mission is to provide our customers with a unique car buying experience, where they can find the car of their dreams and receive personalized customer service.
